# OctoAcme Role Onboarding Checklist

## Purpose
Standardize how new team members are onboarded into their roles within OctoAcme projects. This checklist ensures clarity of responsibilities, relationships, and expectations.

## Pre-Onboarding (Manager)
- [ ] Review role description in `octoacme-roles-and-personas.md`
- [ ] Identify key cross-functional partners for the role
- [ ] Prepare introduction meetings with stakeholders
- [ ] Gather relevant project artifacts and documentation
- [ ] Set up access to tools (project boards, repos, communication channels)

## Week 1: Role Clarity & Context

### Day 1-2: Orientation
- [ ] Meet with direct manager to understand project context
- [ ] Review OctoAcme Project Management Overview
- [ ] Understand project's business objectives and success metrics
- [ ] Review current project status and risks

### Day 3-5: Role Deep Dive
- [ ] Review detailed role description and responsibilities
- [ ] Understand your role in the project lifecycle (Initiation → Release)
- [ ] Review role-specific process documents (e.g., Developers → Execution & Tracking)
- [ ] Understand escalation paths and decision authority

## Week 2-3: Relationship Building & Workflow Integration

### Cross-functional Meetings
- [ ] 1:1 with Product Manager (understand priorities and constraints)
- [ ] 1:1 with Project Manager (understand timelines and dependencies)
- [ ] 1:1 with Developers/QA (understand workflow and communication preferences)
- [ ] 1:1 with Security Officer (if applicable to role)
- [ ] 1:1 with key stakeholders (if applicable)

### Process & Tool Orientation
- [ ] Attend daily standup and learn team rhythm
- [ ] Set up project board access and learn workflow
- [ ] Learn how your role participates in sprint planning
- [ ] Review communication templates and status reporting expectations
- [ ] Understand documentation requirements for your role

## Week 3-4: First Contribution & Feedback

### Initial Assignments
- [ ] Complete first small task or contribution in your role
- [ ] Shadow or pair with experienced team member if available
- [ ] Receive feedback from manager on first deliverables

### Knowledge Check
- [ ] Can you explain your role's responsibilities clearly?
- [ ] Do you know who to escalate issues to?
- [ ] Do you understand how decisions are made?
- [ ] Can you identify key artifacts for your role?
- [ ] Do you know when/how to participate in ceremonies?

### Feedback Session
- [ ] Debrief with manager on progress
- [ ] Identify gaps or questions
- [ ] Schedule ongoing 1:1s for support

## Ongoing: Continuous Learning

- [ ] Monthly role check-ins with manager
- [ ] Quarterly review of role responsibilities and improvements
- [ ] Participate in retrospectives to learn from team
- [ ] Seek mentorship on role-specific best practices
- [ ] Contribute to process improvements

## Resources to Share

- [ ] Link to `docs/octoacme-roles-and-personas.md`
- [ ] Link to relevant process documents (e.g., octoacme-execution-and-tracking.md)
- [ ] Project One-pager
- [ ] Risk register and decision log
- [ ] Communication plan and stakeholder list
- [ ] Team working agreements and definitions of done
- [ ] Links to key tools (project board, repo, Slack channel, etc.)

---

## Manager Notes

- Schedule onboarding 1:1s in advance to ensure availability of key stakeholders
- Use this checklist as a conversation guide, not just a task list
- Encourage questions and feedback from new team members
- Follow up at 30, 60, and 90 days to ensure role clarity and engagement
